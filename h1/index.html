Plasma Passage is the first Hypernatural Island, released during June 2025.



Island biography

Although Plasma Passage resides not far from Mirror Plant Island, the island is hardly accessible. This castle is shielded by a protective bubble, and the only way to get around the bubble is by using The Passage. The Passage is not a physical location, rather a "rip" in reality. Any monster brave enough to enter The Passage will be transported straight to Plasma Passage. The protective bubble also acts like a breeding grounds for Plasma Essence, which can be used to alter the natural monsters who dwell here.
