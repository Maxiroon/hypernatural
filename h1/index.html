Plasma Passage is the first Hypernatural Island, released during June 2025.



Island biography

Although Plasma Passage resides not far from Mirror Plant Island, the island is hardly accessible. This castle is shielded by a protective bubble, and the only way to get around the bubble is by using The Passage. The Passage is not a physical location, rather a "rip" in reality. Any monster brave enough to enter The Passage will be transported straight to Plasma Passage. The protective bubble also acts like a breeding grounds for Plasma Essence, which can be used to alter the natural monsters who dwell here.

Cantrum

2x2 Grid | 500 | Sells for 5,000 | Production (lvl20, 100% happy): 205/h, 600 Max

The plasma essence has altered this natural monster! Cantrums sports a metal pot on it's head. This pot protects a sensitive flower growing from the Cantrum's head that resembles it's natural counterpart. Something Cantrums were not accustomed to as a natural monster were long limbs, so it's common to see one flailing about as they get used to these new appendiges.

Fundae

2x2 Grid | 500 | Sells for 5,000 | Production (lvl20, 100% happy): 199/h, 597 Max

The plasma essence has altered this natural monster! Fundaes' inherit their stumpy fingers from their natural counter part, and not much else. Fundaes are immune to getting brainfreeze, allowing them to consume as much ice cream as their heart desires!

Cristofern

1x1 Grid | 1,000 | Sells for 7,500 | Production (lvl20, 100% happy): 206/h, 619 Max

The plasma essence has altered this natural monster! The rocks that the Cristofern may seem like ordinary rocks, however they are infused with musical abilities which Cristoferns can use. Looking closer at the tendrils found around this monster, they are actually the same as the roots of the Oaktopus!

Maskarade

1x1 Grid | 1,000 | Sells for 7,500 | Production (lvl20, 100% happy): 215/h, 656 Max

The plasma essence has altered this natural monster! What sets apart this hypernatural from its natural counterpart is it's mask and hands. The mask seems to make the already precious natural more charismatic, but who knows what's on the other side?

Plorgob

3x3 Grid | 1,000 | Sells for 7,500 | Production (lvl20, 100% happy): 199/h, 620 Max

The plasma essence has altered this natural monster! When infused with plasma essence, the no-limb natural sports many! One thing that Plorgobs kept as an ethereal is the fact they they have no eyes. Instead, the eyes that are found on the hands are actually critters separate from the Plorgob. They act in harmony, with the critters acting as the Plorgob's eyes, and the Plorgob acting as the critter's body.

Belloe

3x3 Grid | 1,500 | Sells for 10,000 | Production (lvl20, 100% happy): 272/h, 735 Max

The plasma essence has altered this natural monster! The offshoot of a Belloe's horn is constantly growing until eventually it falls out, similar to it's natural counterpart's horns. This shed offshoot is then repurposed as the handle of a Belloe's staff, which holds a strange orb on top of it. No one knows for sure the orb's purpose. It is commoly misunderstood to be a Belloe's eye. We know this isn't true, however, as evident in Belloes' constant bumbling about and the ring of "Look out Belloe!" throughout the Passage.
