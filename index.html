These islands work through a new feature: Ethereal Essences! Essences are used to obtain hypernatural monsters. On the surface, they appear to be the Natural Essences from Dawn of Fire, however the ethereals' instability in the mirror timeline caused these Essences to be imbued with the ethereal elements. Ethereal Essences can created using the Deconstructor! This structure acts as both the castle and the way to obtain Essence. The Deconstructor allows you to spend Shards to conjure up to ten Ethereal Essence at a time. A chart of cost and time can be found at the bottom of the page.

ㅤ

Once you've collected some Ethereal Essence, you can upgrade the breeding structure to become the Infused breeding Structure! This upgrade allows the structure to be able to infuse natural monsters with the ethereal Essence. Here, you can select a monster to infuse the Essence with. The more elements a monster has, the lower the chances of success are. You can increase your chances by using more ethereal Essence.

ㅤ

All five islands take place in the mirror dimension. These islands' main currency is shards, however they can produce both coins and shards. Natural monsters will produce coins and hypernaturals will produce shards.
