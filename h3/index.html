Mech Manor is the second Hypernatural Island, released during February 2026.



Island biography

While the ethereal colossal experimented with the design elements of Mech Islet, some pieces were discarded as they were no longer necessary. These parts were collected by a titan in the depths of the living ocean. Over time, these parts heavily rusted due to the water. That didn't deter the titan from gathering them, however. Soon enough, a mysterious energy summoned the titan, similar to the titan residing in the castle of Mech Passage. The titan emerged from the water, much to the dismay of the nearby Mirror Air Island.
