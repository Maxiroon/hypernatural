Mech Manor is the second Hypernatural Island, released during February 2026.



Island biography

While the ethereal colossal experimented with the design elements of Mech Islet, some pieces were discarded as they were no longer necessary. These parts were collected by a titan in the depths of the living ocean. Over time, these parts heavily rusted due to the water. That didn't deter the titan from gathering them, however. Soon enough, a mysterious energy summoned the titan, similar to the titan residing in the castle of Plasma Passage. The titan emerged from the water, much to the dismay of the nearby Mirror Air Island.

Smoakle

2x2 Grid | 500 | Sells for 5,000 | Production (lvl20, 100% happy): 191/h, 541 Max

The Mech essence has altered this natural monster! The essence caused the air elemental to loose it's wings. To compensate for this, the mech element granted the monster some tracks to steady themselves on the ground. The way the monster now harnesses the air element is through a smoke stack from it's head, which allows it to create new sounds never dreamed of before. With these changes, the Air-mech monster Smoakle was born!

Clangor

2x2 Grid | 500 | Sells for 5,000 | Production (lvl20, 100% happy): 192/h, 610 Max

The Mech essence has altered this natural monster! The cold elemental lost most of its fur, with only some of it remaining on the top of it's head. The essence has also seemed to trigger the monster to grow another set of arms, similar to it's Epic cousin. This monster also seemed to love clanging things together, granting it's name Clangor.

Spi-kah

2x2 Grid | 500 | Sells for 5,000 | Production (lvl20, 100% happy): 196/h, 598 Max

The Mech essence has altered this natural monster! Most of the fire aspect of the fire monster isn't present in the new Hypernatural's appearence, but rather in it's sound! Spi-kahs are able to use their speaker to produce the best beats known to the monster world! They also have a unique ability of recording sounds around them and playing it back. Although, it does play back a little distored.

Gustomp

3x3 Grid | 1,000 | Sells for 7,500 | Production (lvl20, 100% happy): 204/h, 614 Max

The Mech essence has altered this natural monster! The essence allowed the air-cold elemental's beak to transform into a large tube filled with an unknown clear liquid. To maintain a good body temperature in the summer months, this liquid acts as a built-in air conditioning, complete with a fan.

Beetz

2x2 Grid | 1,000 | Sells for 7,500 | Production (lvl20, 100% happy): 209/h, 666 Max

The Mech essence has altered this natural monster! The essence cause the glorious wings of the Air-Fire elemental to shrink drastically, among other changes. Now the hands are allowed to float free from the main body, which now lacks many facial features. Without a mouth to sing, the Beetz uses it's hands to produce some beats. Although this does raise some questions: How do Beetz know where their keyboard is? How do they know what they're playing?

Baloggo

2x2 Grid | 1,000 | Sells for 7,500 | Production (lvl20, 100% happy): 205/h, 636 Max

The Mech essence has altered this natural monster! The essence has caused the loss of the fire elemental's nose and fur. But now they are gifted with incredible arm strength, allowing them to lift their entire body on one leg! The mech essence also gives this monster some mechnical enhancements, sporting a holographic tail.
